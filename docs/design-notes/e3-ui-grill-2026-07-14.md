## Locked

1. **Notice model (replaces the spec's phantom "toast pattern"):** the Ledger is the
   event source. New Ledger kind **`settlement`** (contractId, guildId, met/missed,
   points delta) emitted at each period settlement — the audit trail gains the
   missing "missed" trace (E11 benefits too). The UI keeps a `lastSeenTick` and
   derives notices from events appended since — immune to tick-folding at 10×/100×
   by construction. Presentation: a compact **persistent notice strip** with a count
   badge (click → the board), not a flash-and-gone toast. Rejected: store-emitted
   toast queue (second source of truth, spam at 100×); no notices (kills the
   settlement-day beat).

2. **UI language: Polish everywhere.** New E3 surfaces ship Polish; existing English
   strings (LedgerOverlay, PriceBoardOverlay, FleetList) get rewritten in a separate
   small issue (not blocking the wave). Guild working names go Polish in the spec
   table (flavor, tunable). Recorded in CLAUDE.md (UI strings — Polish). Rationale:
   the game's voice is the owner's language; portfolio-visibility argument for
   English considered and declined.

3. **Cluster B deferred to its own grill after E3** — the Kontrakty tab will change
   what the player needs from the board; redesigning the economic surface before
   playing with contracts is premature. Cluster A (route automation) waits with it.
